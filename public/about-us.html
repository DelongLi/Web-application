Stay Fit Stay Engaged & Connected

Are you looking to get fit but can never seem to get the motivation up? Do you find that going to gym and researching for that perfect routine and meal plan all a big hassle? Stop letting those hinder your progress today, and get engaged with us in this community-based fitness helper and take one step closer to your goal everyday!

This website provides a welcoming and encouraging environment where you can easily get access to all of the following features:

  • Create your own perfect fitness plan

    Based on your personal health information, your preferred fitness style as well as the particular goal that you set, a detailed, customized fitness plan will be generated that fits your needs perfectly.

  • Control your diet through personalized meal plan

    Creating the right meal plan to go along with your workout made easy. Based on the goal that you set for a specific amount of calories that you want to lose, a selection of meal plans will be provided to you along with the detailed instructions to prepare them.

  • Real-time engagement with other fitness lovers

    Discover workout pals within close proximity to you and workout together! Or create live-chat room to check each other's progress and get motivated!

  • Outdoor sporting experience

    Besides indoor fitness routines, outdoor options are also open to choose when selecting your preferred fitness style. Call up your friends to follow a well planned-out outdoor fitness experience, or make new friends along the way!
